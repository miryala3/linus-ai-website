Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I cancel mid-year?

Nothing changes until your paid period ends. You keep full access — all seats, all features — until the last day you have paid for. After that the license expires and you must stop using LINUS-AI or renew.

Do I need the internet to run LINUS-AI?

No. The binary is fully self-contained. License validation can be done offline with our air-gap key option. Contact us if you need fully disconnected operation.

How many machines can I deploy on?

Unlimited. Enterprise covers all machines operated by your organization. Deploy on developer laptops, build servers, inference nodes, and edge devices under a single key.

Can I embed LINUS-AI in products I ship to customers?

Enterprise includes OEM rights for up to 3 internal products. Enterprise Plus extends this to unlimited products with full white-labeling. Contact us for SaaS or multi-tenant redistribution.

Is source code included?

Enterprise covers compiled binaries and internal source access for up to 20 engineers under the commercial license terms. Source escrow is available to Enterprise Plus customers on request.

What payment methods are accepted?

Credit cards and PayPal via the checkout above. For invoiced ACH, wire transfer, or purchase order, email support@linus-ai.com.

Do subsidiaries need separate licenses?

No. Enterprise covers all employees and contractors within your legal entity and its wholly-owned subsidiaries under one key.

What is the 99% SLA for?

The license validation endpoint at license.linus-ai.com. Only relevant for online license checks — air-gap deployments do not rely on the endpoint at all.
