Pricing FAQ

What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept PayPal (including credit/debit cards via PayPal, PayPal balance, and Pay Later). Enterprise customers can pay by bank transfer or invoice. Contact billing@linus-ai.com.
Is there a refund policy? +
Yes. 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. Email billing@linus-ai.com with your order reference.
What happens when a perpetual licence's 1-year update period expires? +
The software continues to run indefinitely — it is perpetual. You can run the version you downloaded forever. You just won't receive new feature updates unless you renew at a discounted upgrade price.
Can I upgrade from Professional to Team? +
Yes. Email billing@linus-ai.com and we'll issue a pro-rated upgrade invoice. Your existing licence key is preserved — no reinstallation needed.
